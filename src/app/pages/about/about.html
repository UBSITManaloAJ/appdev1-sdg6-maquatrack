SDG 3 — Good Health & Well-being

About Marhaba PH

A beginner-friendly gym planner supporting the United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 3 — ensuring healthy lives and promoting well-being for all ages.

🎯 Our Mission

Marhaba PH helps Filipinos build consistent workout habits through structured, science-based workout plans. We make fitness planning simple, accessible, and effective for everyone — from beginners to intermediate gym-goers.

🌍 SDG 3 Connection

SDG 3 aims to ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for all ages. Regular exercise reduces the risk of lifestyle diseases like diabetes, hypertension, and obesity — which are rising in the Philippines. Marhaba PH directly addresses this by making fitness guidance accessible to everyone.

📊 Workout Splits Explained

  • Full Body (2-3 days) — Best for beginners. Train all muscles each session.
  • PPL (3-6 days) — Push, Pull, Legs. Classic and effective.
  • Upper/Lower (4 days) — Great balance of volume and recovery.
  • Bro Split (5 days) — One muscle group per day. Max volume.
  • Advanced PPL (6 days) — PPL twice per week. For dedicated lifters.

🏋️ Fitness Basics

  • Progressive Overload — Gradually increase weight or reps over time
  • Compound Movements — Squats, deadlifts, bench press work multiple muscles
  • Rest Days — Muscles grow during rest, not during training
  • Consistency — Showing up regularly beats perfect workouts
  • Form First — Always prioritize proper form over heavy weight

💪 Goal Guide

  • Build Muscle — 4 sets × 8-12 reps, 60-90 sec rest
  • Gain Strength — 5 sets × 3-5 reps, 3-5 min rest
  • Lose Weight — 3 sets × 15-20 reps, 30-45 sec rest
  • General Fitness — 3 sets × 10-15 reps, 60 sec rest

🇵🇭 Why This Matters

Lifestyle diseases are the leading cause of death in the Philippines. Hypertension affects 1 in 4 Filipinos. Regular exercise is one of the most effective ways to prevent these conditions. Marhaba PH aims to make structured fitness guidance free and accessible to every Filipino.
