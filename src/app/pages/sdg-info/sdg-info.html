SDG 6

Clean Water and Sanitation

Ensure availability and sustainable management of water and sanitation for all.

🎯 The Goal

SDG 6 aims to ensure clean water and sanitation for everyone by 2030. Over 2 billion people still lack access to safe drinking water, and 3.6 billion lack safely managed sanitation facilities.

🇵🇭 Why It Matters in the Philippines

Many communities in the Philippines, especially in rural areas, still struggle with access to clean water. Waterborne diseases remain a leading cause of illness, making this SDG especially critical locally.

📌 Key Targets

  • Universal access to safe drinking water by 2030
  • End open defecation and provide sanitation for all
  • Improve water quality by reducing pollution
  • Protect and restore water-related ecosystems
  • Expand international cooperation on water management

💡 What You Can Do

  • Fix leaks at home — a dripping tap wastes 20L/day
  • Take shorter showers
  • Turn off taps while brushing teeth
  • Collect rainwater for plants
  • Support clean water organizations
