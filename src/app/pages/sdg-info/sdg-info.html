Good Health & Well-being

Marhaba PH supports the United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 3 by promoting fitness, healthy habits, and an active lifestyle for all.

🏃 What is SDG 3?

SDG 3 aims to ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for people of all ages by 2030. It covers physical health, mental wellness, access to healthcare, and prevention of diseases caused by unhealthy lifestyles. Regular exercise and proper nutrition are key pillars of this goal.

🇵🇭 Why It Matters in the Philippines

Lifestyle diseases like diabetes, hypertension, and obesity are rising in the Philippines. Many Filipinos lack access to quality fitness guidance and healthy habit tracking tools. Marhaba PH aims to bridge this gap by making fitness tracking accessible, fun, and motivating for everyone.

🎯 SDG 3 Key Targets

  • Reduce premature mortality from non-communicable diseases
  • Promote mental health and well-being for all ages
  • Strengthen prevention of substance abuse
  • Achieve universal health coverage by 2030
  • Support health research and development globally
  • Reduce the global maternal mortality ratio
